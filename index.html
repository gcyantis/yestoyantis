Our Story

Both Colin and Rachel swiped right on a popular dating app. Colin caught Rachel's interest when he recognized the location of one of her profile pictures (the end of the long walk at Windsor Castle). They realized that they lived close together in Church Hill, so decided to meet at Union Market and see if there was anything to the match. After the world's longest first date that included: walking from one end of Church Hill to the other, desserts at the Hill Cafe, and sitting at the city overlook at Libby Hill, it was clear they were going to have to see each other again. They cooly planned to wait almost a full week before the next date but Rachel got antsy and convinced Colin to walk to the grocery store with her in the rain a few days early. He said yes, and she's been dragging him all over the world ever since.
